How does this work?

Send a referral
Tap the "Email Referral" button and enter the email address of the person you want to refer into the "Referral Email" field, enter your name into the "Your Name" field and if you would like to include any other information enter it into the "Message" field. Once you are done tap the "Submit" button and an email will be sent to the email address provided. An email alert will also be sent to Joey Torkildson for tracking purposes.

Send this app
Tap the "Send this app" button and enter the email address of the person you want to send the app into the "Email" field and enter your name into the "Your Name" field. Once you are done tap the "Submit" button and an email will a link to the apps Landing Page be sent to the email address provided. An email alert will also be sent to Joey Torkildson for tracking purposes.

Get Your Own App
Tap the "Get your own app!" button and enter the email address of the person interested in getting more information about apps from App-Platform.net in the "Email" field. Once you are done tap the "Submit" button and a representative of App-Platform.net will be in touch.
